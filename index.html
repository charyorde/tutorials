Acadaman

Acadaman is a multi-typed learning management system for all formal learning institutions.

Learning Institution Types

Acadaman support various learning institution types including:

  • Primary
  • High School
  • College
  • University
  • Professional
  • Corporates

Student Application Management

Student Application Management involves student enrolment into an educational institution. Acadaman student enrolment process begins at Student Application

Acadaman Student Application workflow makes use of 5 states:

  • Application
  • Rejected
  • Reviewed
  • Submitted
  • Approved

The process uses the following detailed workflow:

  • When a new application is submitted, application status is moved to Reviewed
  • When application status is moved to Reviewed, the applicant role is now set to "Applicant"
  • When an applicant application is reviewed, the applicant is created as a user and login details are sent to user.
  • If an applicant application does not pass the initial review, application state is marked as "Rejected" and related records are deleted from the database.

Grading

Grading is designed to provide incentive reward for achievement and assist in identifying problems of the student.

It is used for analyzing the performance, talent and skills of students. It involves the collection and evaluation of evidence on students' achievement or performance over a specified period of time, such as nine weeks, an academic semester, or entire academic year. Through this process, various types of descriptive information and measures of students' performance are converted into grades or marks that summarize students' accomplishments.

The main advantage is speed, since no manual calculations are needed, and confirmation of grade for any further necessary alterations can also be performed more quickly than traditional means. Managing grades had become easier for instructors when using electronic spreadsheets. It is the important record to keep even for the longest time for the referral and credentials of the student to enter their next level of attaining their goals.

Acadaman supports 2 forms of Grading Systems:

  1. Grading system by local country
  2. WES standard

Fees

Acadaman allows users to pay for fees by using the concept of Fee Structures. A Fee Structure is a pre-defined configuration that structure fee items such as tuition, into payable products using unique attributes such as fee catalog, fee type and instalment type to categorize what exactly users pay for and when they pay for it.

Understanding Fee Types

Acadaman has two built-in fee types.

  • General fee
  • Program-specific fee

Built-in Fee Catalog

A Fee Catalog is a group that a particular fee item belong to. Acadaman has the following built-in Fee Catalog for all learning institution types:

  • Freshman
  • Freshman local
  • Freshman International
  • Local
  • Non-local (That is, from another state of a country)
  • International
