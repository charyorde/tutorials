Manage Acadaman

Once Acadaman is successfully provisioned, you can perform various administrative tasks including:

  • View a list of Admission Applications
  • Add new users and assign roles to them
  • Add Departments/Programmes
  • Add Student Grade
  • Import Scores from legacy systems
  • Attach existing Fee Structures to Students

View Admission Applications

When a new academic session is open, details of potential students who applied through the admission application process can be viewed in a detailed report and exported as deemed fit.

To view the admission application details and status of potential students:

  1. Sign in to Acadaman
  2. Click Manage
  3. From the list of Admission Applications, Click View Application

Available to only University, College, High, Primary, Corporate and Professional Institution types

Add New User

New users can be added to Acadaman without various roles assign to them such as Student, Staff, Applicant, Registrar, Lecturer, HOD, Dean, Senate, Vice-chancellor, Parent, Guardian etc

To add a user:

  1. Sign in to Acadaman
  2. Click **Manage > Users
  3. Enter new user details, assign role(s) and Click Create new account

Add Programme

Available to only University, College, Professional and Corporate Learning Institution types

To add a Programme:

  1. Sign in to Acadaman
  2. Click Configure > Programme > Add Programme
  3. Enter Programme details and click Save

Add Faculty

Available to only College and University Learning Institution types

To add a Faculty:

  1. Sign in to Acadaman
  2. Click Manage School > Faculty > Add Faculty
  3. Enter Faculty details and click Save

Add Department

Available to only University, College and Corporate Learning Institution types

To add a department:

  1. Sign in to Acadaman
  2. Click Manage School > Department > Add Department
  3. Enter Department details and click Save

Add Course

  1. Sign in to Acadaman
  2. Click Manage School > Department > Add Course
  3. Enter course details and click Save

Add Grade (by local country)

Acadaman supports grading system of all world countries.

To use existing local country's grading system

  • Sign in to Acadaman
  • Click **Configure > Grade System
  • Select your country of choice from the Country dropdown widget
  • Click Set Default Country
